Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number is included in your shipment confirmation email. It follows the format MOVVE-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. You can also find it in your MOVVE dashboard under "Active Shipments."

How often is tracking data updated?

Tracking updates in real time for air and ground shipments, and at every port call for ocean freight. You can also enable push notifications for instant milestone alerts.

Can I track multiple shipments at once?

Yes — MOVVE accounts support bulk tracking. Log in to your dashboard to monitor up to 500 shipments simultaneously with custom filters and saved views.

What if my tracking shows no updates?

If your shipment status hasn't changed in 24 hours, contact our 24/7 support team. Some delays occur during customs processing or transshipment — our team will investigate immediately.
